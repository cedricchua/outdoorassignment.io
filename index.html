MacRitchie Nature Trail & Reservoir Park

For those who love the Great Outdoors

MacRitchie Reservoir is the largest of the 4 reservoirs within the Central Catchment Nature Reserve. The others include Lower Peirce Reservoir, Upper Peirce Reservoir and Upper Seletar Reservoir.

This beautiful and lush forest park is a popular destination for runners, water sports enthusiasts and nature lovers. Click the image below for directions to MacRitchie Nature Trail and Reservoir Park!

Runners and trekkers will enjoy the 11-kilometer nature trail loop known as MacRitchie Trails, located within the tropical rainforest of MacRitchie.

Paddle Lodge @ MacRitchie Reservoir allows water sports enthusiasts to try their hand at kayaking and canoeing.

Nature lovers can visit lookout points such as the Jelutong Tower and the Treetop Walk, where you'll get a beautiful view of the forest from above the trees!
