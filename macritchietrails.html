MacRitchie Trails

For runners, trekkers and families

MacRitchie Trails features 6 different routes of varying difficulties! From children to the expert trail runner, all are welcome to get in touch with nature on the MacRitchie Trails!

The Petai, Chemperai, Prunus and Jering boardwalks along the water's edge also provide a more scenic view of the reservoir! If you're feeling adventurous, try out the Pink Route, featuring a 9.5km dirt trail, ending at Rifle Range Road!
